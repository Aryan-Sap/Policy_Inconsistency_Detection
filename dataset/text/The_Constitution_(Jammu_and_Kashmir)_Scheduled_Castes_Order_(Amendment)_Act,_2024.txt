MINISTRY OF LAW AND JUSTICE
(Legislative Department)
New Delhi, the 12th February, 2024/Magha 23, 1945 (Saka)
The following Act of Parliament received the assent of the President on the
12th February, 2024 and is hereby published for general information:—
THE CONSTITUTION (JAMMU AND KASHMIR) SCHEDULED
CASTES ORDER (AMENDMENT) ACT, 2024
(NO. 4 OF 2024)
[12th February, 2024.]
An Act further to amend the Constitution (Jammu and Kashmir) Scheduled
Castes Order, 1956.
BE it enacted by Parliament in the Seventy-fifth Year of the Republic of India as
follows:—
1. This Act may be called the Constitution (Jammu and Kashmir) Scheduled Castes Short title.
Order (Amendment) Act, 2024.
2 THE GAZETTE OF INDIA EXTRAORDINARY [PART II—SEC. 1]
Amendment of 2. In the Schedule to the Constitution (Jammu and Kashmir) Scheduled Castes Order, C.O. 52.
Constitution 1956, for entry 5, the following entry shall be substituted, namely:—
(Jammu and
Kashmir) “5. Valmiki (in the Union territory of Jammu and Kashmir only), Chura, Bhangi,
Scheduled
Balmiki, Mehtar”.
Castes Order,
1956.
