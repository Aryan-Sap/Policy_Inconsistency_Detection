MINISTRY OF LAW AND JUSTICE
(Legislative Department)
New Delhi, the 3rd January, 2023/Pausa 13, 1944 (Saka)
The following Act of Parliament received the assent of the President on the
2nd January, 2023 and is hereby published for general information:—
THE CONSTITUTION (SCHEDULED TRIBES) ORDER
(SECOND AMENDMENT) ACT, 2022
NO. 1 OF 2023
[2nd January, 2023.]
An Act further to amend the Constitution (Scheduled Tribes) Order,
1950 to modify the list of Scheduled Tribes in the State of Tamil Nadu.
BE it enacted by Parliament in the Seventy-third Year of the Republic of India as
follows:—
1. This Act may be called the Constitution (Scheduled Tribes) Order (Second Short title.
Amendment) Act, 2022.
2 THE GAZETTE OF INDIA EXTRAORDINARY [PART II— SEC. 1]
Amendment of 2. In the Constitution (Scheduled Tribes) Order, 1950, in the Schedule, in Part XIV.— C.O. 22.
Constitution Tamil Nadu, after entry 36, the following entry shall be inserted, namely:—
(Scheduled
Tribes) Order, "37. Narikoravan, Kurivikkaran.".
1950.
————
DR. REETA VASISHTA,
Secretary to the Govt. of India.
